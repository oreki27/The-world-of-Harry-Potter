Godric's hollow

Historically important to the wizarding world,
it is the celebrated village
where Voldemort was first defeated

>
Godric's Hollow fact file

WHERE IN THE WORLD:

A village in the West Country of England

RESIDENTS OR OWNERS:

Godric Gryffindor, James and Lily Potter, the Dumbledore family, Bathilda Bagshot

SIGNIFICANT EVENTS THAT TOOK PLACE HERE:

Voldemort was defeated for the first time; Harry returns in an attempt to recover the Sword of Gryffindor, and to visit his parents' graves
