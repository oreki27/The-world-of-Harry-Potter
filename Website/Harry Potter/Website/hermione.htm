Hermione Granger



“Books! And cleverness! There are more important things–Friendship! And Bravery!”
Hermione Granger
Hermione Granger is the cleverest witch of her generation, and so much more than just a sidekick to Harry Potter and Ron Weasley. She regularly gets her wizarding friends out of binds with her smarts, magic abilities, and quick-thinking solutions, even though she has no wizarding blood herself.

Description

Name: Hermione Jean Granger
Birthday: 19th September 1979
Wand: Dragon Heartstring core, ten and three-quarter inches, vine wood
House: Gryffindor
Patronus: Otter
Parents: Mr and Mrs Granger, Both Muggles, both dentists
Skills: Almost Everything
Hobbies: Reading, campaigning for House-Elves' rights
Spouse: Ron Bilius Weasley
