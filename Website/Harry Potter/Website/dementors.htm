Dementors



The guards of Azkaban, they feed on human happiness and can extract souls with their Dementor's kiss

Description

Appearance: Shaped like human covered in dark hooded cloaks, their skin resembles that of a grey rotting body
Magic Abilities: Though they are blind, they can sense and feed on positive feelings, draining their victim's happiness
Typical Habitats: Azkaban
