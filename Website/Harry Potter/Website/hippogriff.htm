Hippogriff

Half Horse, half eagle creatures, immensely proud and extremely dangerous.

Description

Appearance: A cross between a horse and an eagle.
Dangers: Proud creatures prone to attack if they are not shown respect, with rozor sharp beaks and foot long talons.
Allegiance: Loyal when shown proper respect.
