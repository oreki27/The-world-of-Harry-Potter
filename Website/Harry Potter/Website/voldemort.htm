Lord Voldemort



"I'm going to sit here and watch you die, Harry Potter. Take your time, I'm in no hurry."
~Lord Voldemort
Formerly known as Tom Marvolo Riddle and commonly reffered to as He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named. The most powerful dark wizard of all time.

Description

Name: Lord Voldemort, formerly Tom Marvolo Riddle
Birthday: 31st December 1926
Wand: Thirteen and a half inches, yew, phoenix feather, shares its core with Harry Potter's wand
House: Slytherin
Parents: Tom Riddle Sr(Muggle) and Merope Gaunt(witch)
Skills: Dark Magic, Inferi creation and control, Parseltongue and the power to possess living creatures.
Death: 2nd May 1998
