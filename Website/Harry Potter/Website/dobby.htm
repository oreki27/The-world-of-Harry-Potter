House Elves



"Tis part of the house-elf's enslavement, sir. We keep their secrets and our silence, sir, we uphold the family's honour, and we never speak ill of them."
~Dobby
Loyal magical creatures bound to their owners as servants for life.

Description

Appearance: Large heads, long noses and fingers, very short, with bulging, tennis ball eyes and bat-like ears.
Magic Abilities: Their own type of magic performed without a wand which includes the ability to apparate, disarm and make objects leviate.
Allegiance: Bound to serve one owner unless freed by being given clothes by that owner.
