Acromantula



Highly Dangerous giant spiders, of which Hagrid is rather fond

Description

Appearance: Thick black hair-covered bodies, with a legspan reaching up to fifteen feet
Dangers: Untrainable, man-eating and highly dangerous
Magic Abilities: Capable of human speech, and possess near-human intelligence
Typical Habitats: Originating in Borneo, the Acromantula usually inhabits dense jungle
