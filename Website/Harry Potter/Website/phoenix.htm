Phoenix



"It so happens that the phoenix whose tail feather is in your wand, gave another feather- just one other."
~Garrick Olllivander
Faithful magical birds which burst into flame when their body begins to fail, to be reborn from the ashes.

Description

Appearance: Red and Gold feathers
Magic Abilities: Ability to burst into flames when their bodies begin to fail, then be reborn from the ashes. Can carry incredibly heavy loads, tears have healing properties
Allegiance: Domesticated phoenixes are loyal to their owners
